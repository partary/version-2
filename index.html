German Health System

In Germany there are around 1,8 Million people working as nurses. Around 460 000 nurses work in hospitals, 1,2 million work with elder people in the mobile services (they come to your house/apartment) and in the residences. Working time weekly: The regular working time is 38,5 hours („Vollzeit“). But most of the people work less („Teilzeit“), for example 30 hours or 25 per week. For sundays and night work you get extra money (plus 20-50 percent) Nurses are working in a „shift system care“, means they start at 6:00, at 14:00 or at around 20:00 (for the night).

Salary

  • The first regular salary for professional job starters (3 years of nursery school) ist 3 304 Euro per month. Of course taxes and social assurances will reduce it by around 800/900 Euro, depending on the social situation.
  • Average salary for nurses is 3.776 € per month, or 45.312 € per year. The salary increases with special courses or schools (for example intensive care) and of course with the experience (if you work since 5 years or 10 years, the salary is higher as with one year).
  • An assistent nurse (one year school) earns around 2 489 Euro (average).
  • If you are going to start a nurse school in Germany (3 years time, partly school and work in hospitals/residences), you earn around 1 400 Euro per month.

Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications do I need to work as a nurse in Germany?

Qualifications required for nursing in Germany typically include completion of a recognized nursing program, registration or licensure in your home country, and validation of qualifications by German authorities.

Do I need to speak German to work as a nurse in Germany?

Proficiency in German is often required, especially for patient care roles. However, some healthcare facilities may offer language assistance programs or require a certain level of language proficiency before employment.

How does the visa process work for nurses traveling to Germany?

The visa process for nurses varies depending on nationality and individual circumstances. Our agency provides guidance and assistance throughout the visa application process, including gathering required documentation and liaising with immigration authorities.
